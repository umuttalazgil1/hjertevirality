1. Introduction

Welcome to HjerteViralify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authenticate with TikTok, we receive basic profile information as permitted by TikTok's API.

  • Local Storage: We store your preferences and favorites locally in your browser using LocalStorage.
  • Analytics Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our trend tracking service

  • To communicate with you about service updates

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Your favorites and preferences are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly sync them. We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with TikTok's API. When you use our service, you are also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our service or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser LocalStorage to save your preferences and favorites. This data remains on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
